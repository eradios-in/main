About eRadios.in

eRadios.in is India's dedicated online platform for discovering and listening to radio stations from across the country and the world. We bring together a wide range of Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, and Punjabi, covering everything from music and news to devotional content and talk shows. Whether you're in India or abroad, our responsive and fast-streaming platform ensures that you can enjoy high-quality radio anytime, anywhere.

With an ever-growing library of stations, we provide listeners access to regional, national, and international content that reflects the diverse culture of India. Our mission is to support small community radios, mainstream FM channels, and niche broadcasters by offering them visibility on a unified interface. Users can browse by language, genre, or state and enjoy seamless streaming without interruptions. Discover the soundtrack of India—one station at a time.
