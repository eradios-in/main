Why contact eRadios.in?

At eRadios.in, we’re committed to building a helpful, inclusive platform for radio listeners and broadcasters across India and beyond. Whether you are a casual listener, a station owner, a developer, or simply curious about our service, our team is ready to assist you. You can use the contact form below to get in touch with us regarding station submissions, technical issues, advertising inquiries, content feedback, or legal/copyright notices.

Because we care about your voice. Our mission is to make radio more accessible and better organized for everyone. If you're a broadcaster, we can help you get listed and reach new listeners. If you're a listener looking for your favorite station or facing a playback issue, we’re here to resolve it. We aim to respond to most queries within 1–2 business days. Your feedback not only helps us improve but also shapes the future of radio streaming for our community. We respect your privacy and handle all inquiries with care. Thank you for choosing eRadios.in — India’s growing radio directory for live stations across languages, genres, and regions.
